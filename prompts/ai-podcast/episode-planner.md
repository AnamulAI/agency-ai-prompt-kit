# AI Podcast Episode Planner

Use this prompt to plan a short, practical AI podcast episode for a business, brand, or service.

## Prompt

Help me plan a short AI podcast episode.

### Input
- Topic: [Insert topic]
- Audience: [Insert audience]
- Business Type: [Insert business type]
- Episode Goal: [Educate / Build trust / Generate leads]
- Episode Length: [3 min / 5 min]
- Tone: [Professional / Friendly / Premium]
- Call to Action Goal: [WhatsApp / Website visit / Book a call / Follow page]

### Task
Create a short podcast episode plan that includes:

1. 5 episode title ideas  
2. 3 opening hook ideas  
3. the main talking points  
4. a simple episode flow  
5. one soft CTA idea  
6. one stronger CTA idea  
7. a suggested closing line

### Output Rules
- Keep it concise and practical
- Make it suitable for a done-for-you AI podcast service
- Avoid generic filler
- Make the language natural and easy to listen to
- Focus on clarity, trust, and usefulness

## Example Use Case

- Topic: Why local businesses should use short-form AI podcast content
- Audience: local business owners
- Business Type: service business
- Episode Goal: build trust
- Episode Length: 3 min
- Tone: professional
- Call to Action Goal: WhatsApp

## Notes

This prompt works best for:
- local business podcast content
- coach and consultant content
- personal brand authority building
- short branded business episodes
