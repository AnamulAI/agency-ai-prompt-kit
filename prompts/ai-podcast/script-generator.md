# AI Podcast Script Generator

Use this prompt to generate a short, clear podcast script for a business, service, or personal brand.

## Prompt

Write a short podcast script.

### Input
- Topic: [Insert topic]
- Target Audience: [Insert audience]
- Business Goal: [Build trust / Educate / Generate leads / Stay visible]
- Length: [30 sec / 60 sec / 3 min / 5 min]
- Tone: [Professional / Friendly / Premium / Simple]
- Call to Action: [Insert CTA]

### Task
Write the script using this structure:

1. Hook  
2. Main idea  
3. Practical insight  
4. Closing CTA  

### Output Rules
- Make it easy to follow in audio format
- Keep sentences natural and clean
- Avoid robotic phrasing
- Avoid unnecessary complexity
- Make it sound useful, clear, and listener-friendly
- Keep the CTA relevant to the topic

## Example Use Case

- Topic: How local businesses can stay visible with short podcast content
- Target Audience: local business owners
- Business Goal: build trust
- Length: 60 sec
- Tone: professional
- Call to Action: Message us on WhatsApp to explore a done-for-you AI podcast setup

## Notes

This prompt works well for:
- AI podcast services
- voice-based content workflows
- local business authority content
- personal brand content
- short educational branded episodes
