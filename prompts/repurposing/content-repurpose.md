# Content Repurposing Prompt

Use this prompt to turn one core idea into multiple content assets without losing message consistency.

## Prompt

Turn this core idea into multiple content formats.

### Input
- Core Idea: [Insert idea]
- Audience: [Insert audience]
- Offer/Service: [Insert offer]
- Tone: [Insert tone]

### Task
Turn the core idea into:

1. 3 short social posts  
2. 2 carousel ideas  
3. 2 short video hook ideas  
4. 1 email angle  
5. 1 CTA-focused post  

### Output Rules
- Keep the messaging aligned across all formats
- Do not make the outputs repetitive
- Keep the ideas practical and audience-relevant
- Make them suitable for service-based marketing
- Focus on clarity, usefulness, and conversion potential

## Example Use Case

- Core Idea: Most local businesses struggle to stay visible consistently
- Audience: local business owners
- Offer/Service: AI Podcast for Local Business
- Tone: professional

## Notes

This prompt works well for:
- service offer promotion
- content repurposing workflows
- AI podcast marketing
- SEO service marketing
- authority-building content systems
