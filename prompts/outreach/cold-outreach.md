# Cold Outreach Prompt

Use this prompt to write short, natural cold outreach messages that lead with pain, solution, and outcome.

## Prompt

Write a cold outreach message for this service.

### Input
- Service: [Insert service]
- Target Prospect: [Insert prospect type]
- Pain Point: [Insert pain point]
- Solution: [Insert solution]
- Outcome: [Insert result]
- Tone: [Professional / Friendly / Direct]
- Platform: [Email / LinkedIn / Facebook / Instagram]

### Task
Create:

1. 3 outreach message variations  
2. 3 subject line ideas if the platform is email  
3. 2 CTA options  

### Rules
- Do not list random skills or tools
- Lead with the business problem
- Explain the solution simply
- Make the expected outcome clear
- Keep it short, useful, and natural
- Avoid sounding spammy or desperate

## Example Use Case

- Service: Website SEO Optimization
- Target Prospect: local service business owner
- Pain Point: website is not bringing qualified traffic or leads
- Solution: fix key SEO and on-page structure issues
- Outcome: better visibility and more relevant leads
- Tone: professional
- Platform: email

## Notes

This prompt works well for:
- freelancer outreach
- agency lead generation
- service-based offers
- SEO outreach
- AI podcast service outreach
- website and landing page offers
