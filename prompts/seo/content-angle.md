# SEO Content Angle Prompt

Use this prompt to generate content ideas that attract attention, build trust, and support service-based SEO goals.

## Prompt

Give me 10 content ideas for this service.

### Input
- Service: [Insert service]
- Audience: [Insert audience]
- Niche: [Insert niche]
- Goal: [Traffic / Trust / Leads]

### Task
For each content idea, include:

1. title  
2. target keyword angle  
3. why people would care  
4. the likely search intent  
5. a simple CTA direction  

### Output Rules
- Do not make them generic
- Focus on useful, searchable, and trust-building topics
- Keep the ideas relevant to the service and audience
- Make them realistic for a service business blog or content strategy

## Example Use Case

- Service: Website SEO Optimization
- Audience: local business owners
- Niche: service business
- Goal: leads

## Notes

This prompt works well for:
- service business blogs
- SEO content planning
- local SEO content ideas
- authority-building content
- lead-focused content strategies
