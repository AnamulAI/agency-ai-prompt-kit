# Offer Clarity Prompt

Use this prompt to turn a rough service idea into a clear, practical, and easier-to-sell offer.

## Prompt

I want to turn a rough service idea into a clear service offer.

### Input
- Service Idea: [Insert idea]
- Audience: [Insert audience]
- Problem Solved: [Insert problem]
- Delivery Style: [Done-for-you / Consulting / Productized service]
- Expected Outcome: [Insert outcome]
- Differentiator: [Insert differentiator]

### Task
Turn this into a clear service offer.

Include:
1. 5 offer name ideas  
2. what is included  
3. who it is for  
4. what problem it solves  
5. expected outcome  
6. positioning angle  
7. delivery promise  
8. possible CTA  

Then also add:
- the most attractive version for a freelancer or small agency
- the easiest-to-sell version
- 3 simple packaging ideas

### Output Rules
- Keep it specific and practical
- Avoid fluffy wording
- Make it useful for real client work
- Focus on clarity, desirability, and simplicity

## Example Use Case

- Service Idea: done-for-you AI podcast service
- Audience: local businesses
- Problem Solved: inconsistent content and low audience trust
- Delivery Style: done-for-you
- Expected Outcome: regular branded content that builds visibility and trust
- Differentiator: no recording-heavy workflow required

## Notes

This prompt works well for:
- new service creation
- productized offers
- freelancer packages
- agency service positioning
- niche service refinement
